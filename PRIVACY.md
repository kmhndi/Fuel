# Fuel — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 1, 2026_

**No data leaves your device.**

Fuel is a personal calorie and supplement tracker designed to be completely
private: everything you enter stays on your phone.

## What we collect
**Nothing.** Fuel has no account system, no login, and no servers. We do not
collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or usage data.

## Where your data lives
All of your entries — meals, calories, macros, water, caffeine, weight, body
measurements, supplements, reminders, mood check-ins, and settings — are stored
locally on your device in an on-device database. They never leave your phone
unless you explicitly export a backup file and share it yourself.

## Notifications
Fuel can schedule local notifications (e.g. supplement and water reminders)
directly on your device. They are delivered on-device by iOS and are not sent
through any external server.

## Backups you create
If you use the Export feature, Fuel creates a backup file and hands it to your
device's share sheet. What happens to it afterward is under your control.

## Third parties & tracking
Fuel contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

## Children's privacy
Fuel does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children,
because it does not collect information at all.

## Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new date.
