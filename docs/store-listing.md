## Description (≤4000 chars)
Fuel is a calorie, macro and supplement tracker that keeps everything on your phone. No account, no cloud, no ads, no tracking — just a fast, private way to stay on top of your day.

Log a meal in seconds, watch your calorie ring, and see whether today landed in a deficit or surplus. Schedule your supplements and build a streak. It's the whole picture, and it's yours alone.

WHY FUEL
• 100% private — all data stays on your device. No sign-up, no servers.
• Fast logging — anything you eat is saved for one-tap re-adding later.
• Honest energy balance — eaten vs burned, not guesswork.

CALORIES & MACROS
• Animated daily calorie ring against your goal
• Protein, carbs and fat — plus fiber, sugar and net carbs
• Meals grouped by breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks — or your own categories
• Quick-add food library, custom presets, notes and tags
• Copy yesterday, duplicate a meal, or log on any past day

ENERGY BALANCE
• See your daily deficit or surplus: calories in minus calories out
• Add exercise burns and an optional resting burn rate (RMR)
• Built-in goal calculator (TDEE) for lose / maintain / gain targets
• Different calorie goals for different weekdays (training vs rest)

SUPPLEMENTS
• Daily reminders with custom times, specific weekdays, or twice a day
• Check them off and build a streak
• Track doses left and get a refill heads-up

MORE TRACKING
• Water and caffeine with daily goals
• Weight trend, goal weight and body measurements
• Quick mood & energy check-ins

INSIGHTS
• 7-, 14- and 30-day trends with a moving-average line
• Calendar heatmap of your logging and on-target days
• Achievements and streaks to keep momentum

YOURS TO KEEP
• Light and dark themes with a choice of accent color
• Export and restore your data anytime
• No account required — ever

Fuel doesn't sell your data because it never leaves your phone. Track your nutrition the private way.

Fuel is a personal tracking tool and does not provide medical advice.
